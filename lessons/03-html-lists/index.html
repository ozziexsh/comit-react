List of Spells

Alberto
A spell presumably used to open objects such as doors and windows.
Accio
This charm summons an object to the caster, potentially over a significant distance.
Expecto Patronum
Evokes a partially-tangible positive energy force known as a Patronus
Sectumsempra
A dark spell that creates large, blood-oozing gashes on the subject as if said subject had been "slashed by a sward".
Immobulus
Renders living targets immobile.
Partis Temporus
Creates a temporary gap through protactive magical barriers.
Locomotor
The spell causes the named object to rise in the air and move around at the will of the caster.
